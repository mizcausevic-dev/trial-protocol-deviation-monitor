# Kinetic Gain Embedded Tie-Back

`trial-protocol-deviation-monitor` is the Pharma / Life Sciences proof surface for protocol deviations, missing source packets, stale CAPA evidence, and audit-safe review posture.

## What it demonstrates

- deviation intake and routing posture
- owner-safe escalation for missing source, training, and CAPA proof
- inspection prevention through packet completeness visibility
- operator-readable deviation review without exposing live GxP systems

## Why this matters to Kinetic Gain Embedded

Embedded products increasingly need quality-aware and evidence-aware operator surfaces inside customer workflows. This repo shows the reusable primitive:

1. ingest structured trial and packet exports
2. analyze missing proof, stale packets, and workflow drift
3. present one buyer-readable control surface for operators, reviewers, and auditors

The same architecture can be embedded into SaaS products that need:

- deviation workflow visibility
- CAPA and inspection-readiness analytics
- packet-completeness scoring
- safe internal review tooling for life sciences operations
